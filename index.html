GEO641: Changes in Human Development Over Time 2000 - 2018

        
Map displays global changes in human development between the years 2000 and 2018 using the United Nations Human Development Index (HDI). HDI is formulated through the combination of life expectancy, education and gross national income indices.
        Higher values reflect greater levels of development.
